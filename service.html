It's SP's environmental sustainability

We believe in creating safe and green spaces for the well-being of our community

  • SP has installed water-efficient fixtures throughout campus, such as low-flow toilets and faucets. The college has also replaced its lawn with drought-tolerant plants.
  • SP has installed energy-efficient lighting and appliances throughout campus. The college has also implemented a number of energy-saving measures, such as turning off lights when rooms are not in use and setting computers to sleep when they are not being used.
  • SP has implemented a number of waste reduction measures, such as composting food scraps and recycling paper, plastic, and metal. The college has also installed a number of recycling bins throughout campus.
  • SP has made a number of efforts to use sustainable materials in its construction and operations. For example, the college has used recycled materials in the construction of its new library.
